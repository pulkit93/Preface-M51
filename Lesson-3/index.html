Minecraft

Release Date: November 18, 2011

A sandbox game that allows players to build and explore their own worlds made of blocks. It fosters creativity, survival skills, and teamwork in both single-player and multiplayer modes.

Among Us

Release Date: June 15, 2018

A multiplayer social deduction game where players work together on a spaceship while trying to identify the impostors among them. It emphasizes communication, strategy, and deception in a fun, light-hearted setting.

Mario Kart 8 Deluxe

Release Date: April 28, 2017

A fast-paced racing game featuring iconic Nintendo characters and imaginative tracks. Players can race against each other or AI, using power-ups to gain an advantage and compete for first place.
